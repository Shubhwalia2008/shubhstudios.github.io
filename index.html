About Me

I’m Shubh — I live in the world behind the scenes and on stage: running lights, mixing sound, capturing cinematic frames, bringing rhythm with the dhol, and moving crowds as a DJ. My craft blends technical precision with artistic instinct. Whether I’m programming a lighting sequence, shaping the energy of a room through audio, or dropping a beat that transforms the atmosphere, I’m always building experiences people feel. For me, performance isn’t just a moment — it’s an environment. It’s the emotion in the lights, the pulse in the speakers, the story in a photograph, and the cultural heartbeat of the dhol. Everything I do is about creating that atmosphere where people forget the world for a second and just live in the moment.
